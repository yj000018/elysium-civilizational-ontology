# 06 — Worldchanging Coverage Test (Updated)

## Purpose

Worldchanging (Alex Steffen, 2006) provides a comprehensive grass-roots catalog of sustainability solutions organized into 7 categories. It serves as a concrete "coverage test" for the Elysium ontology. The addition of Lot H (Missing Models) ensures all implicit domains in Worldchanging are now explicitly backed by theoretical models.

---
