# 10 — Universal Analysis Matrix

## Purpose

Provide a reusable matrix to analyze any domain, facet, or sub-facet within the Elysium ontology. This matrix structures the narrative from the current state through crisis to future transformation.

---

## The 12-Step Universal Matrix (Refined)

Based on the research (especially transition management, systems theory, and Worldchanging), the provisional 12-step structure has been refined for maximum analytical power.

### Phase 1: Definition & Importance
**1. What is it? (Ontology)**
- Core definition.
- Where does it sit in the Elysium framework? (Scale, Foundation, Facet)
- What are its key sub-facets?

**2. Why it matters (Teleology)**
- What fundamental human/civilizational need does it fulfill?
- What happens if it fails?

### Phase 2: The Current Paradigm & Crisis
**3. Today's Paradigm (Status Quo)**
- How is this domain currently structured? (e.g., centralized, extractive, hierarchical)
- What are the dominant mental models and assumptions?

**4. Current Crisis / Limitations (The Breakdown)**
- Why is the current paradigm failing?
- What are the systemic limits, externalities, or injustices?
- *Leverage point analysis (Meadows): Where is the system stuck?*

### Phase 3: The Transition
**5. Key Transitions Underway (The Shift)**
- What are the major macro-trends forcing change?
- What is shifting from X to Y? (e.g., ownership to access, linear to circular)

**6. Technology / AI Disruption (The Catalyst)**
- How are AI, computation, and new technologies transforming this domain?
- Is technology centralizing or decentralizing power here?

**7. Consciousness / Cultural Shift (The Inner Transition)**
- How are values, worldviews, and meaning shifting in this domain?
- What inner development is required to manage the outer complexity?

### Phase 4: The Future Paradigm
**8. The Future Paradigm (The Synthesis)**
- What does the transformed, mature state of this domain look like?
- How does it integrate technology, ecology, and human flourishing?

**9. Transition Pathways (The Bridge)**
- What are the practical steps from Phase 2 to Phase 4?
- What policies, movements, or economic shifts are required?

### Phase 5: Action & Reality
**10. Projects / Experiments (The Vanguard)**
- Concrete examples of the future paradigm already existing today.
- *Case studies, organizations, models.*

**11. Risks / Shadows (The Warning)**
- What are the failure modes of the transition?
- What are the dystopian possibilities? (e.g., techno-authoritarianism, greenwashing)

**12. Resources (The Library)**
- Key books, reports, datasets, and thinkers for this domain.

---

## Testing the Matrix: Example "Energy"

| Step | Application to "Energy" (Foundation Layer) |
|------|--------------------------------------------|
| 1. What is it? | The capture, storage, and distribution of power. |
| 2. Why it matters | Master resource. Enables all physical and economic activity. |
| 3. Today's Paradigm | Centralized, fossil-based, extractive, one-way grid. |
| 4. Current Crisis | Climate change, geopolitical vulnerability, resource depletion. |
| 5. Transitions | Fossil → Renewable; Centralized → Distributed. |
| 6. Tech/AI Disruption | Smart grids, AI demand management, decentralized storage. |
| 7. Consciousness Shift | Viewing energy as a commons; ecological awareness. |
| 8. Future Paradigm | 100% renewable, distributed, resilient, zero-marginal-cost. |
| 9. Transition Pathways | Carbon pricing, grid modernization, subsidy shifts. |
| 10. Projects | Denmark wind, Tesla virtual power plants. |
| 11. Risks/Shadows | Mineral bottlenecks, energy poverty, grid hacking. |
| 12. Resources | IRENA, IEA Net Zero, Vaclav Smil. |

**Conclusion:** The 12-step matrix is robust, scalable, and provides a perfect narrative arc for books, articles, or AI generation.
