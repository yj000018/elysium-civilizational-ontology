# 07 — Fractal Scale Test

## Purpose

Test whether the 7 emergent layers function coherently across three scales:
- **Human** (individual)
- **Society** (collective/institutional)
- **Civilization** (planetary/species-level)

---

## Layer 1: Matter / Foundation

| Scale | Expression | Examples |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| **Human** | Body, physical health, shelter, personal safety, material security | Housing, clothing, food access, physical fitness |
| **Society** | Energy systems, water infrastructure, housing stock, transport, material resources | Power grids, water treatment, road networks, building stock |
| **Civilization** | Planetary resource base, energy regime, global infrastructure paradigm, material footprint | Fossil→renewable transition, global logistics, space infrastructure |

**Works across all scales:** YES
**Limitations:** At civilization scale, "foundation" becomes more abstract (resource paradigms vs. concrete objects)
**Confidence:** 5/5

---

## Layer 2: Life / Vitality

| Scale | Expression | Examples |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| **Human** | Personal health, nutrition, vitality, biological functioning, reproductive health | Diet, exercise, sleep, immunity, aging |
| **Society** | Public health systems, food systems, agriculture, biodiversity, ecosystem health | Healthcare, farming, fisheries, forests, pollution control |
| **Civilization** | Planetary biosphere health, species survival, regenerative capacity, Gaia homeostasis | Climate stability, mass extinction, ocean health, soil depletion |

**Works across all scales:** YES
**Limitations:** None significant. The biological/living dimension scales naturally.
**Confidence:** 5/5

---

## Layer 3: Agency / Power

| Scale | Expression | Examples |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| **Human** | Personal agency, competence, career, creativity, entrepreneurship, self-efficacy | Skills, work, income, projects, innovation |
| **Society** | Economic systems, labor markets, industry, innovation ecosystems, value creation | GDP, employment, startups, R&D, trade |
| **Civilization** | Global economic paradigm, technological capacity, productive power, resource allocation | Capitalism/post-capitalism, automation, AI economy, space economy |

**Works across all scales:** YES
**Limitations:** "Power" connotation may be misread as political power (which belongs to Layer 5)
**Confidence:** 5/5

---

## Layer 4: Connection / Cohesion

| Scale | Expression | Examples |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| **Human** | Relationships, love, belonging, social identity, emotional bonds | Family, friendship, partnership, community membership |
| **Society** | Social capital, cultural institutions, art, media, collective identity, trust | Museums, festivals, social media, civil society, traditions |
| **Civilization** | Global cultural exchange, shared narratives, intercultural dialogue, collective identity | UNESCO, internet culture, world religions, shared myths |

**Works across all scales:** YES
**Limitations:** "Culture" is very broad at society level. May need sub-layering.
**Confidence:** 5/5

---

## Layer 5: Expression / Governance

| Scale | Expression | Examples |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| **Human** | Self-expression, communication, personal boundaries, voice, authenticity | Speech, writing, art, assertiveness, personal governance |
| **Society** | Governance systems, law, democracy, media, institutional coordination, justice | Parliament, courts, journalism, regulation, police |
| **Civilization** | Global governance, international law, planetary coordination, civilizational decision-making | UN, ICC, climate agreements, WTO, internet governance |

**Works across all scales:** YES
**Limitations:** The "expression" aspect (individual voice) and "governance" aspect (collective coordination) feel somewhat different at human scale vs. society scale. The link is that both involve organizing/communicating intent.
**Confidence:** 4/5

| Scale | Expression | Examples |

## Key Findings

1. **All 7 layers work across all 3 scales.** The fractal hypothesis is confirmed.

2. **Strongest fractal coherence:** Layers 1-4 (Material, Life, Agency, Connection) scale most naturally.

3. **Slight tension:** Layer 5 (Expression/Governance) has a dual nature — personal expression vs. institutional governance. The unifying principle is "organizing and communicating intent."

4. **Slight tension:** Layer 7 (Consciousness/Meaning) is most concrete at individual scale and most abstract at civilization scale. This is expected — consciousness is inherently interior.

5. **Naming recommendation:** Layer names should use words that work at all scales. Current names are adequate but could be refined:
   - "Foundation" works better than "Matter" at all scales
   - "Vitality" works better than "Life" (avoids confusion with "life" as existence)
   - "Agency" works better than "Power" (avoids political connotation)
   - "Cohesion" works better than "Connection" at society/civilization scale
   - "Governance" works better than "Expression" at society scale (but "Expression" is better at human scale)
   - "Vision" works better than "Knowledge" at civilization scale
   - "Meaning" works better than "Consciousness" at society scale
