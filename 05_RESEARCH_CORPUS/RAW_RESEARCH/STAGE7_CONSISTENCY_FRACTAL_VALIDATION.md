## 2. Fractal Validation Test

2. **Vitalité:** Physiological health, microbiome, connection to local nature.
3. **Agentivité:** Personal labor, income, capacity to create value, skills.

**Result:** Pass. The 7 Foundations perfectly map human existence.

5. **Gouvernance:** City council, police, municipal courts, civic participation.
6. **Vision:** Universities, research centers, urban planning (prospective).
7. **Conscience:** The "soul" of the city, its historical purpose, its shared values.
**Result:** Pass. The 7 Foundations perfectly map urban existence.

### Test Subject C: The Global Civilization
