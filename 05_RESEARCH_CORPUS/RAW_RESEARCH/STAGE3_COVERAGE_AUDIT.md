## Key Findings

### 1. The ontology is well-grounded
Every Foundation has multiple models providing strong backing. The 7-layer structure is not arbitrary — it reflects genuine clustering in the literature.

### 2. Material and ecological layers are specialist-backed
FOUNDATION and VITALITÉ are covered by fewer general models but have deep specialist support (energy frameworks, permaculture, planetary boundaries). This is a feature, not a bug — Elysium's contribution is precisely to elevate these dimensions to Foundation status.

### 3. CONSCIENCE is the most contested layer
Models diverge most on meaning, spirituality, and civilizational direction. This is expected — it's the layer where worldview differences are most acute. Elysium's inclusion of this layer is validated by the sheer number of models that address it, even if they disagree on content.

### 4. Specific gaps to monitor
- **Régénération** as a distinct concept (vs. ecology generally)
- **Identité** as distinct from culture
- **Pensée Systémique** as distinct from science
- These are Elysium's most original structural claims and deserve explicit justification in the book.
