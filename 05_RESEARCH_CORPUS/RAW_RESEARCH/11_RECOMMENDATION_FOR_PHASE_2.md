# 11 — Recommendations for Phase 2 (Synthesis & Design)

## Executive Summary
The ontological research phase is complete. 111 models across 7 domains were analyzed. **The central hypothesis is strongly validated:** there is a universal, fractal, 7-layer structure to human, social, and civilizational development. The data is clean, the clusters are robust, and the framework is ready for Phase 2 design.

## 1. Architecture Recommended for Phase 2

**Adopt Option D: The Fractal Model (3 Scales × 7 Foundations)**

**The 3 Scales:** Human, Society, Civilization
**The 7 Foundations:**
1. Foundation (Material Base)
2. Vitality (Life/Health)
3. Agency (Economy/Production)
4. Cohesion (Community/Culture)
5. Governance (Coordination/Law)
6. Vision (Knowledge/Foresight)
7. Consciousness (Meaning/Purpose)

## 2. What the Research Strongly Validates

- **The 7-Layer Progression:** The movement from material → vital → agential → social → structural → cognitive → conscious is present in dozens of independent traditions (Maslow, Chakras, Spiral Dynamics, Integral, Systems Theory). It is not an arbitrary invention.
- **The Fractal Nature:** The same 7 layers can accurately describe an individual human, a city, or a planetary civilization.
- **Worldchanging Coverage:** The 7 layers easily absorb the entire Worldchanging catalog without distortion.

---

## 3. What the Research Invalidates / Corrects

- **Invalidates flat lists:** Models like the SDGs (17 goals) or Better Life Index (11 domains) are flat and lack an evolutionary logic. Elysium's layered approach is vastly superior for understanding *how* change happens.
- **Corrects the "Technology" problem:** Technology is not a separate layer. It is a tool that exists in Layer 3 (Agency/Production) and Layer 6 (Vision/Knowledge).
- **Corrects the "Ecology" problem:** Ecology is not just "the environment" (Layer 1). It is the living system (Layer 2) and the ultimate planetary context.

---

## 4. Domains Requiring Careful Handling in Phase 2

- **Art & Culture:** Does art belong in Layer 4 (Cohesion/Community) or Layer 5 (Expression) or Layer 7 (Meaning)? *Recommendation: Place Culture in Layer 4, and individual Expression in Layer 5.*
- **Communication vs. Media:** *Recommendation: Communication (the act) is Layer 5 (Governance/Coordination), Media (the cultural product) is Layer 4.*
- **Wisdom vs. Knowledge:** *Recommendation: Knowledge/Science is Layer 6. Wisdom/Ethics/Purpose is Layer 7.*
