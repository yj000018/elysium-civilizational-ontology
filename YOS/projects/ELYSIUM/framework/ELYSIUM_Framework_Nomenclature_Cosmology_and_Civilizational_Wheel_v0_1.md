# ELYSIUM Framework — Nomenclature, Cosmology & Civilizational Wheel

> **Status Note:**
> This document is canonical for the current general architecture, cosmological framing, and terminology decisions approved so far.
> It is not yet final canon for the exact names and contents of:
> * the 7 Organes Civilisationnels
> * the 12 Inquiry Gates
> * the 38 Points de Contact au Réel
>
> Those must be fully recovered from existing ELYSIUM ontology work before finalization.

## 1. Executive Summary

The ELYSIUM Framework is a civilizational cartography and symbolic architecture. It is not merely a descriptive model or a list of categories; it is a living map designed to structure, analyze, and orient civilizational transition. This document canonizes the current validated nomenclature, the underlying cosmology, the visual metaphor of the Civilizational Wheel, and the strict terminology usage rules that govern the framework.

## 2. The Core Proposition of ELYSIUM

ELYSIUM is not only a book. It is a civilizational framework, a symbolic system, and an architecture of transformation.

We are entering a transition where old structures — work, capitalism, institutions, scarcity, sovereignty, education, health, governance — can no longer absorb the new forces alone. A new civilizational map is needed. ELYSIUM proposes this map.

The deeper proposition is the complete reorganization of civilization around:
* new flows
* new civilizational organs
* new institutions
* a new relationship to work
* a new relationship to the living world
* a new relationship to consciousness
* a new relationship to intelligence
* a new relationship to meaning

ELYSIUM is a civilizational cartography. It must not be framed simply as a futurist tech book, an AI book, a utopian manifesto, a political program, a spiritual doctrine, a design system, or a logo exploration.

## 3. Why a New Civilizational Map Is Needed

The current paradigm is facing structural collapse because its underlying operating system is misaligned with the reality of complex, interdependent planetary systems. We cannot navigate the transition using the same cartography that created the crisis. ELYSIUM provides a new map that integrates the material, the vital, the social, the cognitive, and the spiritual into a single coherent architecture.

## 4. The Current Framework Summary

The framework is formalized as a progression representing different orders of reality:

* **0 — CORE**: Centre vide, Mystère, vacuité génératrice et résorbatrice, alpha-oméga, source et destination.
* **1 — Le Tout / The Whole**: La Roue civilisationnelle complète.
* **2 — Spanda**: Mouvement de respiration kosmique : création-résorption.
* **3 — Les 3 Sphères d’Incarnation**: Individu, Société, Civilisation. Trois sphères transparentes imbriquées, comme des atmosphères de complexité croissante. La conscience y prend forme à différents degrés de complexité.
* **5 — Les 5 Flux**: (Provisional: Les 5 Flux Primordiaux) Sens, Information/Intelligence, Énergie, Vivant, Matière.
* **7 — Les 7 Organes Civilisationnels**: Les grandes fonctions vivantes par lesquelles une civilisation tient, respire, se coordonne, se transforme et s’incarne.
* **12 — ELYSIUM Inquiry Process**: 12 Inquiry Gates (les gates / 12 Portes d'Investigation).
* **38 — Couronne d’Application**: Périphérie active, points de contact et d’appui au réel (38 Points de Contact au Réel / les 38 Applications).
* **1 Horizon — Horizon civilisationnel**: Direction finale, non comme utopie fixe, mais comme orientation civilisationnelle.

This sequence (0, 1, 2, 3, 5, 7, 12, 38) is not decorative numerology. It is a symbolic, ontological, cosmological, analytical, and visual architecture.

## 5. The Cosmology of 0 / 1 / 2

### 0 — CORE
The CORE is the empty hub of the Civilizational Wheel. It is the generative and resorptive void, the Mystery, the alpha and omega. It is not merely "Meaning" or "Consciousness," nor is it a theological object or a graphic dot. The void of the hub is what allows the wheel to be a wheel; without the empty center, the wheel is only a disk. The CORE must be represented as empty whenever possible. It is agnostic and non-religious, yet mystical and profound.

### 1 — Le Tout / The Whole
The Whole is the full Civilizational Wheel, the complete mandala, the total architecture that includes the CORE, Spanda, Spheres, Fluxes, Organs, Inquiry Process, Crown, and Horizon. 1 is not the central point alone; it is the totality of the system.

### 2 — Spanda
Spanda is the kosmic respiration, the pulsation of creation-resorption, emanation-return. It is the living movement between void and form. Through Spanda, the CORE breathes the world into form and receives form back into Mystery.

## 6. The 3 Sphères d’Incarnation

**Official concept:** 3 — Les 3 Sphères d’Incarnation (Narrative usage: les Sphères)

1. Individu
2. Société
3. Civilisation

These are not stacked strata or a rigid hierarchy. They are nested, transparent, interpenetrating fields where consciousness takes form at different degrees of complexity.

*Canonical phrase:* L’individu vit dans la société, la société vit dans la civilisation, et la civilisation se réalise à travers les individus et les sociétés.

## 7. The 5 Flux

**Official concept:** 5 — Les 5 Flux (Recommended provisional: Les 5 Flux Primordiaux)

The 5 Flux are the transition layer between non-form and form. They are the cosmological engine of ELYSIUM, corresponding to archetypal elemental principles.

Order from subtle to dense:
1. Sens — Éther
2. Information / Intelligence — Air
3. Énergie — Feu
4. Vivant — Eau
5. Matière — Terre

*Required formulation:* Du vide naît la respiration. De la respiration émergent les cinq flux. Les cinq flux densifient le réel du Sens vers la Matière, puis résorbent la Matière vers le Mystère.

## 8. The 7 Organes Civilisationnels

**Official concept:** 7 — Les 7 Organes Civilisationnels

*Definition:* Les 7 Organes Civilisationnels sont les grandes fonctions vivantes par lesquelles une civilisation tient, respire, se coordonne, se transforme et s’incarne.
*Structural definition:* Les grandes fonctions structurantes à travers lesquelles les 5 Flux prennent forme dans les 3 Sphères d’Incarnation et se déploient dans la Couronne d’Application.

They are living functions, like organs in a body, not inert blocks or foundations underneath the system.

## 11. The Horizon Civilisationnel

**Official concept:** 1 Horizon — Horizon civilisationnel

The Horizon is the direction of the framework. It is not a fixed utopia or a closed blueprint, but an orientation toward greater integration, consciousness, regeneration, and meaning.

## 12. Relations Between Components

*   **CORE** generates the **5 Flux** through the movement of **Spanda**.
*   The **5 Flux** take form within the **3 Sphères d'Incarnation** via the **7 Organes Civilisationnels**.
*   The **7 Organes** deploy concretely into the **38 Points de Contact au Réel** on the **Couronne d'Application**.
*   The **12 Inquiry Gates** are the methodological lens applied to any part of this structure.
*   The entire system (the **1 Whole**) rolls toward the **Horizon civilisationnel**.

## 13. The Civilizational Wheel as Master Image

The Civilizational Wheel is the core visual metaphor and the symbolic architecture of a civilization in movement.

*   **CORE** = empty hub
*   **5 Fluxes** = transition between non-form and form
*   **7 Organes Civilisationnels** = appear visually as rays
*   **38 Points de Contact** = appear on the Crown of Application
*   **The Wheel as a whole** = The Whole / Le Tout
*   **Spanda** = movement of the wheel / breathing of the wheel
*   **The Spheres** = nested fields in which the whole model incarnates
*   **Horizon** = orientation of the wheel

## 15. Rejected / Deprioritized Terms

*   **Fondations:** Rejected as official for the 7 because it suggests a base underneath the system, whereas the 7 are living organs inside the wheel.
*   **Jante:** Rejected as official for the application layer because it is too mechanical / vehicular.
*   **Échelles:** Deprioritized for the 3 because it suggests linear zoom or hierarchy rather than nested incarnation.
*   **Questions:** Deprioritized for the 12 because "questions" is too flat; "Gates" implies passage and transformation.
*   **Facettes:** Less preferred for the 38 because it can feel abstract compared to Points de Contact au Réel.
*   **Sens / Conscience as center:** Rejected as exclusive CORE label because the CORE must remain the Mystery / empty generative void.

## 16. Visual Representation Rules

*   **Empty center:** The CORE must be visually empty.
*   **Nested transparency:** The 3 Spheres should be transparent and nested, not a pyramid.
*   **Transitional flux layer:** The 5 Flux should appear between the empty CORE and the formed structure.
*   **Living radial organs:** The 7 Organes appear as rays connecting the center to the periphery.
*   **Application crown:** The 38 Points de Contact sit on the active periphery (the Crown).
*   **Noble aesthetic:** The visual language must be noble, symbolic, agnostic, non-religious, and profound.
*   **Logo abstraction:** The future logo should be an elegant abstraction of this wheel, not a miniature diagram.
