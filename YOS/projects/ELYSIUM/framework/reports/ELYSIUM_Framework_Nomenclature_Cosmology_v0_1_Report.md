## 7. Terminology Decisions
*   **0:** CORE (not just Meaning/Consciousness).
*   **1:** Le Tout / The Whole (the full wheel).
*   **2:** Spanda (respiration kosmique).
*   **3:** 3 Sphères d'Incarnation (not Échelles).
*   **5:** 5 Flux Primordiaux (provisional).
*   **7:** 7 Organes Civilisationnels (not Fondations).
*   **12:** ELYSIUM Inquiry Process / 12 Inquiry Gates (not Questions).
*   **38:** Couronne d'Application / 38 Points de Contact au Réel (not Jante).
*   **Horizon:** Horizon civilisationnel.

## 8. Rejected / Deprioritized Terms
*   *Fondations* (too static/base-like)
*   *Jante* (too mechanical)
*   *Échelles* (implies linear hierarchy)
*   *Questions* (too flat)
*   *Sens/Conscience* as the exclusive label for the CORE (closes the Mystery).

## 9. Visual Representation Rules Captured
*   Empty center (CORE).
*   Nested transparency (Spheres).
*   Transitional flux layer.
*   Living radial organs (rays).
*   Application crown at the periphery.
*   Noble, agnostic, symbolic aesthetic.

## 10. Risks / Ambiguities
*   Legacy documents still heavily use "Fondations" and "Facettes". A comprehensive find-and-replace or mapping strategy will be needed for older manuscript drafts and program state files to avoid confusion.
*   The 5 Flux naming needs final validation from the Founder.
