# ELYSIUM Book Master Content Plan

**Status:** CANONICAL
**Phase:** Phase III-0B
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-27

## Purpose
This document defines the central architecture of the ELYSIUM book. It bridges the gap between the 126 integrated models (ontology) and the narrative experience of the reader (manuscript).

## What is the book?
ELYSIUM is a comprehensive civilizational ontology presented as a narrative journey. It is not merely an encyclopedia; it is an analytical framework that diagnoses the pathologies of the current civilization and maps the structural vectors for transition toward a regenerative future.

## Who is the reader?
The primary reader is the "Architect of the New Society"—policymakers, systems thinkers, founders, community leaders, and visionaries who require a rigorous, holistic model to guide transition efforts.

## What transformation does the reader undergo?
From fragmented, siloed understanding of global crises to a unified, systemic comprehension of civilizational mechanics (the 7 foundations) and the actionable levers of transition.

## Major Parts
1. **01_opening:** The diagnosis of the meta-crisis and the introduction of the civilizational ontology.
2. **02_foundations:** The core of the book, detailing the 7 foundations and 38 facets.
3. **03_transition_dynamics:** How the foundations interact and shift from pathological to regenerative states.
4. **04_implementation:** The practical application of the ontology for architects and builders.
5. **05_appendices:** Methodological notes, the 12-step matrix, and index of models.

## Chapters / Foundations
- **F01 Material Base:** Energy, Water, Habitat, Infrastructure, Mobility, Materials
- **F02 Vitality:** Health, Food & Agriculture, Biodiversity, Ecosystems, Regeneration
- **F03 Agency:** Economy, Work, Finance, Entrepreneurship, Innovation, Production
- **F04 Cohesion:** Community, Relationships, Culture, Art, Media, Belonging & Identity
- **F05 Governance:** Law & Justice, Democracy, Institutions, Security, Communication
- **F06 Vision:** Education, Science, Foresight, Systems Thinking, Technology
- **F07 Consciousness:** Ethics, Spirituality, Meaning & Purpose, Worldview, Civilizational Direction

## Architectural Translation (Ontology to Narrative)
The 7 foundations and 38 facets provide the structure. The 12-Step Universal Analysis Matrix provides the analytical lens. **Crucially: We do not mechanically create 456 modules (38 facets × 12 steps).** 
- **Facet = conceptual unit** (e.g., Energy)
- **Module = narrative unit** (e.g., "The Energy Trap: EROI and Civilizational Complexity")
- **Matrix steps = metadata** ensuring complete analytical coverage across the modules within a facet.
