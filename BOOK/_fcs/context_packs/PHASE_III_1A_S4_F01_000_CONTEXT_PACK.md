## Foundation 01 — Core Argument
Civilization begins in material constraint.
The first condition of any civilization is not ideology, governance, money, or culture.
It is the capacity to organize flows of energy, water, matter, bodies, tools, structures, and movement across space and time.
Material Existence is the substrate through which all higher functions become possible.
The current material foundation of industrial civilization is pathological: organized as a linear extraction engine (extract → process → consume → discard).
A regenerative civilization must reorganize Material Existence into a circular, resilient, adaptive, life-compatible metabolism.
