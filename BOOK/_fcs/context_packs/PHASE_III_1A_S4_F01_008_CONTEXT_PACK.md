5. The transition: Material Existence provides the physical substrate. But civilization is not only matter. The next foundation — Vitality — asks: what animates the material substrate? What makes it alive?

## Key idea
Material Existence is the necessary substrate of civilization. But substrate alone does not constitute life. The question of what animates matter — what makes civilization vital, not merely functional — is the question that opens Foundation 02.

## Suggested closing direction
"Matter is the condition of civilization. But condition is not character. What makes civilization vital — what gives it the capacity to sustain, adapt, and renew — is not material alone. It is the subject of the next foundation."
