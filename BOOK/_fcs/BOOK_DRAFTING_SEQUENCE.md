# ELYSIUM Book Drafting Sequence

**Status:** CANONICAL
**Phase:** Phase III-0B
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-27

## Purpose
This document outlines the sequential strategy for drafting the actual prose of the ELYSIUM manuscript.

## The Principle of Iterative Deepening
We do not draft the book linearly from page 1 to page 500 in a single pass. We draft in iterative layers to ensure structural integrity and voice consistency before committing to massive word counts.

## Sequence

### Phase III-1: The Opening Draft (Tone & Stakes)
- **Target:** `01_opening`
- **Objective:** Establish the voice, the diagnosis of the polycrisis, and introduce the ontology.
- **Why first?** It sets the stylistic benchmark for the rest of the book.

### Phase III-2: The Material Baseline (F01)
- **Target:** `02_foundations/F01_material_base`
- **Objective:** Draft the modules covering Energy, Water, Habitat, Infrastructure, Mobility, and Materials.
- **Why second?** It grounds the highly theoretical ontology in hard, undeniable physical realities, building reader trust before moving to abstract concepts.

### Phase III-3: The Biological and Economic Engines (F02 & F03)
- **Target:** `F02_vitality` and `F03_agency`
- **Objective:** Draft the modules covering life systems and human economic organization.

### Phase III-4: The Social and Governance Structures (F04 & F05)
- **Target:** `F04_cohesion` and `F05_governance`
- **Objective:** Draft the modules covering culture, community, law, and institutions.

### Phase III-5: The Metaphysical Peak (F06 & F07)
- **Target:** `F06_vision` and `F07_consciousness`
- **Objective:** Draft the modules covering education, foresight, ethics, and civilizational purpose.

### Phase III-6: Dynamics and Application (Parts 03 & 04)
- **Target:** `03_transition_dynamics` and `04_implementation`
- **Objective:** Synthesize the foundations into a dynamic model of change and provide the practical manual for architects.

### Phase III-7: Global Review and Refinement
- **Target:** Entire Manuscript
- **Objective:** Cross-referencing, smoothing transitions, ensuring the 126 models are properly cited, and final stylistic polish.

## AI Routing for Drafting
- **Preparation:** Manus executes action requests to prepare context.
- **Drafting:** Claude API is invoked for all long-form prose generation.
- **Review:** ChatGPT Chief Architect reviews the draft against the scaffold constraints of the ontology.
