# Master Content Layer

The Master Content Layer is the primary output of FCS. It is a structured source corpus, not a final designed publication.

## Components

The Master Content Layer includes:
- structured manuscript nodes
- source prose
- summaries
- reader promises
- concepts
- resources
- relations
- graph metadata
- review status
- AI action history
- rendered working views
- handoff-ready structured outputs

FCS stops at the Master Content Layer. Downstream systems may later transform it into books, websites, presentations, pitches, courses, social snippets, or knowledge interfaces. Phase III-0A does not create those downstream products.
