# Review Model

Review in FCS operates on multiple levels: module-level, node/facet-level, chapter/foundation-level, part-level, full corpus, resource, graph, and handoff readiness.

## Core Principle
Write locally with AI. Review globally through rendered views. Correct at the source.

## Review Responsibilities
- **Claude**: May review style and readability.
- **ChatGPT Chief Architect**: Reviews architecture, canonical alignment, argument structure, scope discipline, and integration readiness.
- **Founder**: Reviews strategic direction, taste, tone, major locks, and go/no-go decisions.
