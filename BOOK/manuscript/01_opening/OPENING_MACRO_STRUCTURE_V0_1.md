## 5. Critical Structural Decision

**Do NOT fully explain the 7 Foundations in the Opening.**

The Opening reveals:
- that there are 7 Foundations
- why they are ordered
- how they are read
- why they form an ascent from material base to consciousness
- why the architecture matters

The Opening does NOT provide mini-chapters for each Foundation.
Detailed foundation exploration belongs in Part 02.
