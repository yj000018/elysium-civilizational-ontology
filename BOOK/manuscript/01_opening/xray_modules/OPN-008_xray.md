# Executive Summary

**EN:** This module introduces the central conceptual reframe of the book: civilization as a living metabolic system. A metabolism takes in inputs, transforms them, regulates its own processes, produces outputs, and maintains itself against entropy. This frame applies to civilization at all scales. It is the central pivot of the Opening Part.

**FR:** Ce module introduit le recadrage conceptuel central du livre : la civilisation comme système métabolique vivant. Un métabolisme prend des intrants, les transforme, régule ses propres processus, produit des extrants et se maintient contre l'entropie. Ce cadre s'applique à la civilisation à toutes les échelles. C'est le pivot central de la Partie Ouverture.

# Beats

**EN:**
- Introduce the metabolic concept: inputs, transformation, regulation, outputs, entropy
- Apply it to civilization at the material level
- Show that metabolism is not just biological — it is a general systems concept
- Introduce the thermodynamic grounding (Prigogine / dissipative structures — optional depth)
- Bridge to OPN-009: metabolism includes symbolic and conscious flows

**FR:**
