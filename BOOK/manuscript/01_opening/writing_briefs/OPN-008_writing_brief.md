## 6. Module Purpose

Deploy the full metabolic frame for civilization. Use metabolism as: inputs, transformation, regulation, outputs, entropy management. Thermodynamic grounding light. Avoid biology-only interpretation. Critical module — do not split.

---

## 7. Opening Direction

> **Directional only — not a finished sentence.**

Open by naming what the machine metaphor could not capture — then introduce metabolism as the frame that can. Begin with the simplest metabolic concept: inputs and transformation.

---

## 8. Closing Direction

> **Directional only — not a finished sentence.**

Close by establishing that civilization is a metabolic entity — and that this changes everything about how we diagnose, understand, and respond to civilizational dynamics

---

## 9. Three Key Concepts to Deploy

1. **Metabolic inputs:** What civilization takes in: energy, matter, information, narratives, consciousness — the five classes of civilizational flows
2. **Metabolic transformation:** How civilization processes inputs: institutions, practices, technologies, cultural forms
3. **Metabolic regulation and entropy management:** How civilization maintains coherence and manages disorder — the regulatory function

---

## 10. Two Traps to Avoid

1. **Biology-only interpretation:** Metabolism is not just biology — it is a structural concept applicable to any complex adaptive system. Avoid reducing to biology.
2. **Splitting the module:** Do not split this module — it must carry the full metabolic frame as a unified argument
