## 9. Three Key Concepts to Deploy

1. **Ontology as map of dependencies:** Not philosophical ideology — a practical map of what exists and how things depend on each other
2. **Civilizational dependencies:** The structural dependencies that hold civilization together — what depends on what
3. **The need for an ontological foundation:** Why a shared map of dependencies is necessary for integrated civilizational diagnosis

## 10. Two Traps to Avoid

2. **Ideology confusion:** Make clear that ontology is a map, not a political or philosophical ideology
