## Drafting Brief

The Opening Part must accomplish four things in sequence:

1. **Hook** (M01): The reader must feel the weight of the polycrisis — not as doom, but as a diagnostic.
2. **Demolish** (M02): The reader must understand why siloed expertise cannot solve the meta-crisis alone.
3. **Reframe** (M03): The reader adopts a new lens — civilization as a metabolic entity.
4. **Map** (M04): The reader receives the 7-Foundation architecture as a navigational promise.
