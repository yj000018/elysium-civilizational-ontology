# Materials

## Function in the Book
To establish the physical limits of extraction and the necessity of circular material flows.

## Intended Argument
A linear economy (extract → use → discard) is a thermodynamic death sentence for civilization.

## Drafting Strategy
Use concrete examples: rare earths, phosphorus, sand. Connect to F01_01 (energy cost of extraction) and F03 (economic incentives for circularity).
