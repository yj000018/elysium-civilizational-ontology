# F01: Material Base

## Executive Summary
The Material Base is the hard physical substrate of civilization. It encompasses the extraction, processing, and distribution of energy, water, food, shelter, mobility, and materials. It is the thermodynamic engine that makes all higher-order complexity possible.

## Function in the Book
To ground the reader in reality. Before discussing economics, culture, or consciousness, we must establish the physical laws and metabolic limits that constrain human ambition.

## Reader Promise
You will see civilization not as an abstract economy, but as a physical metabolism. You will understand why infinite growth on a finite planet is a thermodynamic impossibility, and how we must redesign our material systems to be circular and regenerative.
