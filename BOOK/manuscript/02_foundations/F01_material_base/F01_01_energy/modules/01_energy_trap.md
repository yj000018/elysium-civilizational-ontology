# The Energy Trap

## Function of this Module
To diagnose the core physical limitation of our current civilization: the declining Energy Return on Investment (EROI).

## Intended Argument
Civilization is a heat engine. As EROI declines, more energy must be spent simply acquiring energy, leaving less for societal complexity.
