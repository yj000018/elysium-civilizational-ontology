# F04: Cohesion (Community & Culture)

## Executive Summary
Cohesion addresses Community, Relationships, Culture, Art, Media, and Belonging. It is the layer where individual agency aggregates into collective identity.

## Function in the Book
To show that solving the material and economic crises is impossible without repairing the social fabric.

## Reader Promise
You will understand how to build resilient communities and design cultural narratives that support regenerative transitions.

## Facet List
- F04_01 Community
- F04_02 Relationships
- F04_03 Culture
- F04_04 Art
- F04_05 Media
- F04_06 Belonging & Identity

## Drafting Strategy
Focus on the concept of "social capital" not as a metric, but as the immune system of a civilization. Contrast hyper-individualism with regenerative interdependence.
