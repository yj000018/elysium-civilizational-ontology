# F03: Agency (Economy & Production)

## Executive Summary
Agency explores the systems of human effort: Economy, Work, Finance, Entrepreneurship, Innovation, and Production. It is the engine of value creation.

## Function in the Book
To deconstruct the current financialized economy and propose a system where capital serves life, rather than life serving capital.

## Reader Promise
You will learn how to redesign economic incentives to align with the thermodynamic realities of F01 and the biological realities of F02.

## Facet List
- F03_01 Economy
- F03_02 Work
- F03_03 Finance
- F03_04 Entrepreneurship
- F03_05 Innovation
- F03_06 Production

## Drafting Strategy
Critique the abstraction of finance. Ground economic theory in real-world production and human flourishing. Use the term "software" to describe economic systems running on the "hardware" of F01.
