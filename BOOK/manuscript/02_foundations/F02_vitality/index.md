# F02: Vitality (Life & Ecology)

## Executive Summary
Vitality moves the ontology from the physics of F01 to the biology of F02. It covers human health, food systems, biodiversity, ecosystems, and the overarching principle of regeneration.

## Function in the Book
To establish that civilization is not built *on* nature, but *within* nature. It bridges the gap between raw materials and human agency.

## Reader Promise
You will see health and agriculture not as industries, but as the metabolic interfaces between the human body and the planetary body.

## Facet List
- F02_01 Health
- F02_02 Food & Agriculture
- F02_03 Biodiversity
- F02_04 Ecosystems
- F02_05 Regeneration

## Drafting Strategy
Use biological and ecological metaphors. Emphasize the interconnectedness of human microbiomes with soil microbiomes. Move the reader from an extractive mindset to a regenerative one.
