# Foundation 01: Material Existence

## Executive Summary

Foundation 01 establishes the physical substrate of civilizational existence. It examines the seven material facets — Energy, Water, Habitat, Infrastructure, Mobility, Food, and Materials — as the irreducible physical flows without which no civilizational function is possible.

## Function in the Book

To ground the metabolic model introduced in the Opening in concrete physical reality. Before vitality, agency, or consciousness can be examined, the material basis must be established.
