# F01-000 — Foundation 01 Overview: Material Existence

## Purpose
Open Foundation 01 and establish Material Existence as the first civilizational foundation.

## Must include
1. Why civilization begins in material constraint.
2. Why material systems are not merely "infrastructure" but the substrate of civilization.
3. The six provisional F01 facets: Energy, Water, Habitat, Infrastructure, Mobility, Materials.
4. The core pathology of linear extraction: extract → process → consume → discard.
5. The regenerative direction: circular, resilient, adaptive, life-compatible material metabolism.
6. Bridge to Energy as the first facet.

## Hard constraints
- Do not become a full summary of all F01 modules.
- Do not overdevelop Vitality (F02).
- Do not become a generic climate introduction.
- Do not list the full 38 Facets.
- Keep Opening continuity but move clearly into formal ontology.

## Key idea
Material Existence is the substrate through which all higher civilizational functions become possible.

## Suggested closing direction
The first question of Material Existence is therefore the first question of civilizational metabolism itself: what powers the system?
