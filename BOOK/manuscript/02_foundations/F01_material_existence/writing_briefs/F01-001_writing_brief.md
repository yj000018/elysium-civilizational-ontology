# F01-001 — Energy: The Power Flow
## Purpose: Establish energy as the enabling flow of civilization.
## Must include: 1. Energy as power condition. 2. Historical sequence: human/animal labor, biomass, fossil fuels, electricity, computation. 3. Fossil fuels and industrial expansion. 4. Energy density and complexity. 5. Pathology of fossil dependency. 6. Replacing supply without redesigning demand. 7. Rebound effects. 8. Infrastructure lock-in. 9. Regenerative direction. 10. Bridge to Water.
## Key idea: Energy is not one sector among others. It is the power flow that allows all other sectors to exist.
## Closing: "But power alone is not life. The next material constraint is older than industry, older than cities, older even than agriculture: water."
