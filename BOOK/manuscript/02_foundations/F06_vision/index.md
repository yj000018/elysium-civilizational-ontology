# F06: Vision (Knowledge & Foresight)

## Executive Summary
Vision encompasses Education, Science, Foresight, Systems Thinking, and Technology. It is the sensory and learning apparatus of civilization.

## Function in the Book
To explain how a civilization perceives reality, learns from mistakes, and anticipates the future.

## Reader Promise
You will understand the difference between data and wisdom, and how to build educational and scientific systems that foster systemic thinking rather than narrow specialization.

## Facet List
- F06_01 Education
- F06_02 Science
- F06_03 Foresight
- F06_04 Systems Thinking
- F06_05 Technology

## Drafting Strategy
Focus on epistemological humility. Science is not just about producing technology (F01/F03), but about understanding limits (F02). Emphasize the urgent need for Systems Thinking as a core educational pillar.
