# F07: Consciousness (Meaning & Purpose)

## Executive Summary
Consciousness is the metaphysical peak of the ontology. It covers Ethics, Spirituality, Meaning & Purpose, Worldview, and Civilizational Direction.

## Function in the Book
To answer the ultimate question: What is all this material extraction, economic production, and governance actually *for*?

## Reader Promise
You will see that the meta-crisis is fundamentally a crisis of meaning. You will understand how to align structural action with deep spiritual and ethical purpose.

## Drafting Strategy
This is the most delicate chapter. It must remain rigorous and grounded while discussing the metaphysical. Avoid vague new-age terminology. Connect spiritual alignment directly to systemic action (e.g., "A regenerative economy requires a worldview that does not see nature as dead matter").
