# F05: Governance (Coordination & Law)

## Executive Summary
Governance covers Law & Justice, Democracy, Institutions, Security, and Communication. It is the layer where collective decisions are made and enforced.

## Function in the Book
To redefine politics as systemic coordination. How do we manage the commons in a complex, interconnected world?

## Reader Promise
You will see how our current institutional architecture is mismatched to the complexity of our crises, and how to design agile, resilient governance structures.

## Facet List
- F05_01 Law & Justice
- F05_02 Democracy
- F05_03 Institutions
- F05_04 Security
- F05_05 Communication

## Drafting Strategy
Use cybernetic language. Talk about feedback loops, signal-to-noise ratios in communication, and the speed of institutional adaptation versus the speed of ecological change.
