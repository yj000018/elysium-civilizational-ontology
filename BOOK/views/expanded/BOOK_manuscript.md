# F01: Material Base

## Executive Summary
The Material Base is the hard physical substrate of civilization. It encompasses the extraction, processing, and distribution of energy, water, food, shelter, mobility, and materials. It is the thermodynamic engine that makes all higher-order complexity possible.

## Function in the Book
To ground the reader in reality. Before discussing economics, culture, or consciousness, we must establish the physical laws and metabolic limits that constrain human ambition.

## Reader Promise
You will see civilization not as an abstract economy, but as a physical metabolism. You will understand why infinite growth on a finite planet is a thermodynamic impossibility, and how we must redesign our material systems to be circular and regenerative.

## Facet List
- F01_01 Energy
- F01_02 Water
- F01_03 Habitat
- F01_04 Infrastructure
- F01_05 Mobility
- F01_06 Materials

## Matrix Coverage Strategy
The modules within this foundation will diagnose the current linear, extractive pathology (Steps 1-4), explain the physical models that govern these systems (Steps 5-8), and propose the regenerative transition vectors (Steps 9-12).

## Narrative Role
The foundation sets the stage. It is the "heavy" chapter that establishes the stakes of the meta-crisis.

## Drafting Strategy
Keep it grounded. Use clear physical examples (e.g., EROI - Energy Return on Investment, the water cycle, concrete production). Avoid economic abstractions here; stick to joules, liters, and tons.
